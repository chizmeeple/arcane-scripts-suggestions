Yaggababble

Storyteller Phrases & Suggestions

About Yaggababble

The Yaggababble is a Townsfolk character who receives information about evil players each night. They learn either the number of evil players or the number of evil players who voted for the same player as them.

Night Phase Phrases

"You wake up and see [number] evil players in the game."
When revealing the number of evil players
"You learn that [number] evil players voted for the same player as you today."
When revealing voting information
"Your ability reveals that there are [number] evil players in the game."
Alternative phrasing for number of evil players

Clarification Phrases

"This information is current as of tonight."
When they ask about timing of information
"You don't know which specific players are evil, only the count."
When they ask for specific player names
"The voting information refers to today's vote, not previous days."
When clarifying voting timing
